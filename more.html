More Fun in the Austin Area

The Austin Zoo

A non-profit zoo full of rescued animals. A peaceful experience just outside the city.
(half day)

Pedernales

Beautiful, shallow waterfalls await you at the end of a mellow hike.
(full day)

Mount Bonnell

Defined on google as a "prominent point". Offers a lovely view of the city, 360 bridge, and Lake Austin.
(couple hours)

Lake Austin

Ideally, you have a friend in town with a speed boat. Otherwise rent your SUP and get out there!
(half-to-full day)
